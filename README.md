# Paul Carey & Sons Ltd — Company Website

The official website for **Paul Carey & Sons Ltd**, one of the Isle of Man's leading civil engineering and groundwork contractors. A Manx family business based at Ballasalla Farm, Jurby, founded in 1999 and operating for over 25 years.

## About the Business

Paul Carey & Sons Ltd was founded by Paul Carey and remains family-run, with **Aaron Carey** serving as Projects Director. The company employs over 55 local staff and has built a strong reputation for delivering high-quality civil engineering, groundwork, and environmental services across the Isle of Man.

### Services

- **Civil Engineering** — River works, bridge reconstruction, and highway regeneration.
- **Plant Hire** — High-quality, fully maintained machinery including Hitachi 360 tracked machines for groundwork and construction projects.
- **Haulage & Plant Transport** — Heavy plant transport, aggregate haulage, and oversized load handling.
- **Landscaping & Ponds** — Hard and soft landscaping, pond construction, ground preparation, and drainage solutions.
- **Land Reclamation** — Coastal and marine reclamation, brownfield redevelopment, and environmental improvements.
- **Recycling** — Sustainable aggregate and green waste recycling, site waste management, and soil screening.
- **Demolition** — Structural demolition, site clearance, and controlled deconstruction services.

### Company Values

- **Safety & Health** — Rigorous health, safety, and environmental standards on every project.
- **Family Values** — A family-run business with deep community roots and a commitment to the local workforce.
- **Sustainability** — Environmental responsibility through recycling, waste management, and sustainable practices.
- **Excellence** — 25+ years of delivering high-quality civil engineering and groundwork solutions.
- **Reliability** — Stability and longevity in service delivery, building lasting client relationships.
- **Community** — Supporting local communities and investing in staff development.

## Project Structure

This is the company website built with **Next.js 16**, **TypeScript**, and **Tailwind CSS**.

```
app/
├── page.tsx                  # Homepage
├── about/                    # About the company
├── services/                 # Services overview
├── civil-engineering/        # Civil Engineering service page
├── plant-hire/               # Plant Hire service page
├── haulage/                  # Haulage & Plant Transport service page
├── landscaping/              # Landscaping & Ponds service page
├── land-reclamation/         # Land Reclamation service page
├── recycling/                # Recycling service page
├── demolition/               # Demolition service page
├── quote/                    # Get a Quote page
├── contact/                  # Contact page
├── privacy-policy/           # Privacy Policy
└── terms/                    # Terms & Conditions

components/
├── layout/
│   ├── Navbar.tsx            # Site navigation
│   └── Footer.tsx            # Site footer
└── ui/
    ├── HeroSection.tsx       # Hero banner component
    ├── ServiceCard.tsx       # Service card component
    ├── StatsSection.tsx      # Stats/metrics section
    └── CTASection.tsx        # Call-to-action section
```

## Tech Stack

- **Framework** — Next.js 16 (App Router)
- **Language** — TypeScript
- **Styling** — Tailwind CSS
- **Animations** — Framer Motion
- **Icons** — Lucide React

## Getting Started

```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) to view the site.
